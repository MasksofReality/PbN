## Who They Are

Faithful Hunters carry genuine conviction in a higher power — their connection to the God-Machine's mandate runs through the framework of that faith rather than around it. This is not naivety. Faithful Hunters have seen the worst that exists in this world. Their faith is not innocent — it is forged. They believe because they have seen enough to know that giving up costs more than continuing.
