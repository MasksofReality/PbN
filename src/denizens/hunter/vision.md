## Who They Are

Vision Hunters were not just awakened by the God-Machine — they were shown something. Visions, impressions, glimpses of events that haven't happened yet or are happening somewhere else right now. The Moderators left something in them that stays open, that keeps transmitting. This is a gift and a burden in roughly equal measure — they know things before they materialize, and they see suffering they can't always prevent.

## Creed Abilities

| Level | Option A | Option B |
|---|---|---|
| ●○○○○ | **Premonition** — never surprised, always acts in the first initiative segment. Passive. | **Read Intent** — perceives a conversation partner's immediate intent — lie, attack, flee. Passive. |
| ●●○○○ | **Vision of the Present** — sees/hears a previously visited location in real time, up to 10 minutes. Roll Perception + Alertness (diff 6). | **Sense the Web** — perceives the causal connections between people and events in the current scene. Roll Intelligence + Investigation (diff 7). |
| ●●●○○ | **Flash Forward** — glimpses a likely 24-hour outcome of the current situation. Roll Perception + Occult (diff 7). | **Trace the Thread** — follows a consequence backward to its cause, up to a month past. Roll Intelligence + Investigation (diff 8). |
| ●●●●○ | **Cascade Vision** (2 Conv) — perceives 2–3 branches of a decision point's likely consequence. Roll Perception + Occult (diff 8). | **Broadcast** (2 Conv) — shares a vision directly with allies present, experienced as genuine memory. |
| ●●●●● | **The Full Sight** (3 Conv) — perceives the full causal web of a situation; exhausting, -2 dice next scene. | **Prevent** (3 Conv) — takes one action made possible by certain foreknowledge, once per session. |
