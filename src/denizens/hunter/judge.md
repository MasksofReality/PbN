*Those Who Police All — Including Their Own*

<p class="eyebrow">← Back to <a href="/denizens/hunter/">Hunter</a></p>

## Who They Are

Judge Hunters believe that without accountability, every form of power — human, supernatural, or God-Machine — eventually becomes corrupt. They're the internal affairs of the Hunter community, ensuring Hunters don't become what they hunt, and enforce the God-Machine's mandate on everyone, including wayward Hunters. Judges interface directly with the Society of Leopold's oversight functions, the Veil Keepers' intelligence operations, and the Shadow Directorate's enforcement arm — every faction treats a Judge with careful respect.

## Mundane Hunter Benefits (All Levels)

| Level | Benefit |
|---|---|
| 1 | Formal Society of Leopold oversight standing — may demand cooperation from other Hunters |
| 2 | -1 difficulty on Investigation and Intimidation rolls against Hunters and supernaturals |
| 3 | Can formally censure another Hunter — temporary loss of Society standing and resources, given evidence |
| 4 | Formal verdicts treated as binding by the Society and respected by most of the supernatural community |
| 5 | Can invoke the God-Machine's direct attention on a Consensus violation, escalating its response one stage faster |
