## Who They Are

Underground Hunters prefer to work alone — not from antisocial instinct, though some have it, but because the work goes better when no one knows what they're doing or who they're doing it for. They move through the Basilica's gray areas with practiced ease, collecting information and taking action without leaving a trail anyone can follow. They're the Hunters most likely to know things no one else in the community knows — and most likely to act on those things without telling anyone first.
