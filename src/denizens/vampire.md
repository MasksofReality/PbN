When Caine awoke and walked into Oblivion, he attempted to drag all vampirekind with him. Lilith intervened. Every vampire of that age was given a choice: accept rebirth through Lilith's covenant and become **Lilum**, or follow Caine into the dark. Those who chose rebirth survived. Those who did not are gone.

In-world, characters may call themselves Vampire, Kindred, or Lilum interchangeably — all are accurate. Lilum retain the core nature of vampires — the hunger, the night, the Blood — but carry Lilith's mark rather than Caine's curse. The old Sects (Camarilla, Sabbat, Anarch) no longer exist. The Factions have replaced them entirely.

## The Six Common Bloodlines

| Bloodline | Nature |
|---|---|
| **[Ventrue](/denizens/vampire/ventrue/)** | Lords of will and command. Survivors by nature, leaders by instinct — gravitate toward the Ashen Court. |
| **[Toreador](/denizens/vampire/toreador/)** | Artists, sensualists, and obsessives. They find beauty even in the broken world and use it as both shield and weapon. |
| **[Malkavian](/denizens/vampire/malkavian/)** | Seers touched by madness — or wisdom so vast it looks like madness. Their insight is invaluable; their stability is not guaranteed. |
| **[Gangrel](/denizens/vampire/gangrel/)** | Survivors above all else. Closer to beast than any other bloodline — thrived during the decade of animal regression in ways others cannot imagine. |
| **[Nosferatu](/denizens/vampire/nosferatu/)** | Monstrous in form, indispensable in function. They know every secret in the Basilica because no one watches what they cannot bear to look at. |
| **[True Brujah](/denizens/vampire/true-brujah/)** | Philosophers and warriors of cold, precise fury. Unlike their extinct Brujah cousins, they never succumb to frenzy — they choose their violence deliberately. |

## Strengths of Being Lilum

- **Blood Healing** — Spend 1 Blood Point to heal 1 Bashing health level, or 2 to heal 1 Lethal level. Aggravated damage requires a full night's rest plus 5 Blood Points.
- **The Third Awakening** — Characters embraced before Gehenna who chose Lilith's covenant receive a one-time free reroll during their first full session, representing the clarity of rebirth.
- **Unnatural Toughness** — All Bashing damage is halved (round down).
- **The Beast** — Lilum retain access to frenzy. Controlled frenzy (Willpower expenditure, difficulty 8) adds 2 dice to all Physical pools for its duration.

## Weaknesses of Being Lilum

- **Sunlight** — Direct sunlight deals 1 Aggravated damage per turn; indirect sunlight deals 1 Lethal per turn.
- **Fire** — Deals Aggravated damage as always.
- **The Daysleep** — Lilum are effectively dead during daylight unless awakened by danger. Forced wakefulness costs 1 Blood Point per hour.
- **Blood Dependency** — Reaching 0 Blood Points triggers immediate hunger frenzy.
- **Vitae Bonding** — Three consecutive feedings from the same Lilum creates a full Blood Bond.
- **Stake Through the Heart** — Renders a Lilum immobile, not destroyed.

## House Rules

- No Sects exist. Don't reference Camarilla, Sabbat, or Anarch structures as active organizations in your background.
- The Blood still functions — Disciplines, the Hunger, and the need for blood are unchanged from core rules unless otherwise noted.
- Lilum may feed on any living being but must maintain the Veil. Exposure while feeding is a serious violation.
- Caine and his direct childer are gone. Don't write Antediluvians into your background as active characters.
