## Who They Are

Before the End, the Furies focused their justice primarily on women and children. After the End, after watching the whole world face ruin, they expanded their mandate. The whole world is worth protecting now. Gorgon, their totem, was once unjustly treated by a more powerful spirit — she calls the Furies to stand against all forms of injustice, wherever it is found, at whatever cost. Gender is no longer a prerequisite for deserving justice or for joining the tribe.

## Black Fury Tribe Gift Path

**●○○○○ Heightened Rage** *(Passive)* — Rage rises instinctively when witnessing injustice, bullying, abuse of power, or harm to the vulnerable: +1 die on all Physical rolls for the scene.

**●●○○○ Sense Injustice** *(Passive)* — feel the emotional residue of harm or wrongful treatment done to a place or person touched, as a directional impression pointing toward the cause.

**●●●○○ Avenging Strike** *(1 Rage, diff 6)* — while genuinely fighting to defend or avenge a specific harm, all attacks deal aggravated damage regardless of form or weapon.

**●●●●○ Gorgon's Gaze** *(2 Gnosis, diff 8)* — meeting the gaze of a being who has committed genuine injustice forces a Willpower roll (diff 8) or temporary immobilization against the Fury and those they protect for the scene.

**●●●●● Eshtarra's Justice** *(3 Gnosis, diff 9)* — calls down formal judgment on a being with documented, witnessed injustice: for one month, their worst transgression is visible as a spiritual brand to all Garou and most supernaturals.
