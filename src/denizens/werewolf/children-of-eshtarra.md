## Who They Are

The Children of Eshtarra, formerly the Children of Gaia, renamed themselves at the moment of their rebirth — deliberately. They are not Gaia the Elder's children recovered; they are Eshtarra the Younger's children, made anew with a new purpose. Where the old tribe sought peace because the world was worth preserving, the Children of Eshtarra seek peace because the world must be rebuilt, and you cannot build while you are fighting each other. They are the diplomats and healers of the Garou community, holding open the conversation when other tribes have stopped listening.

## Children of Eshtarra Tribe Gift Path

**●○○○○ Sense Wyrm** *(Passive)* — corruption and spiritual damage register as a physical sensation, automatically, within their vicinity.

**●●○○○ Mother's Touch** *(1 Gnosis, diff 6)* — lay hands on a living being, heal 1 Lethal (or 2 Bashing) level. Cannot self-target.

**●●●○○ Calm** *(1 Gnosis, diff 7)* — speaking in Eshtarra's voice drops all Rage in the scene by 2 for every Garou present.

**●●●●○ The Living Earth** *(2 Gnosis, diff 7)* — establishes a sacred space carrying protection for one week: doubled healing, corruption cannot take root, hostile spirits need a successful opposed Gnosis roll to enter.

**●●●●● Eshtarra's Embrace** *(3 Gnosis, diff 9)* — channels Eshtarra directly for one scene: all allies auto-heal 1 damage per turn; Wyrd corruption in the area suppressed for a week after. The Child spends the next 24 hours in exhausted recovery.
