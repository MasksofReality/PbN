## Who They Are

The Fianna remember. Of all the tribes, their ancestors held onto the most during the decade the Garou were gone — not memories exactly, but the emotional shapes of what mattered. When Eshtarra restored them, the Fianna's first act was to begin talking, to make it into stories, to preserve it before it could be lost. They are the historians and bards of the Garou people, and they celebrate beauty because they know exactly what it costs.

## Fianna Tribe Gift Path

**●○○○○ Persuasion** *(Passive)* — +2 dice to all Social rolls involving persuasion, inspiration, or storytelling.

**●●○○○ Faerie Light** *(1 Gnosis, diff 5)* — a warm light makes all within it feel safe; Social rolls in the light at -1 difficulty; hostile intent feels instinctively unwelcome.

**●●●○○ Balm of the Goddess** *(1 Gnosis, diff 6)* — a performance recovers 1 lost Willpower for the audience and reduces Frenzy risk by 2 for the scene. Requires at least a full minute of performance.

**●●●●○ Howl of the Banshee** *(2 Rage, diff 7)* — a howl carrying grief and fury shakes hostile beings within earshot who fail a Willpower roll (diff 8): -2 dice for the scene, cannot spend Willpower to enhance aggression.

**●●●●● Song of the Heroes** *(3 Gnosis, diff 8)* — tells the story of the greatest deed personally witnessed with such power it briefly lives again: every allied Garou gains 2 Rage, heals 1 Lethal level, +2 dice defending the community for the rest of the scene.
