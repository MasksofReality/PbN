## Who They Are

Trolls are the largest and physically most formidable of all the Kiths, and they know it. What distinguishes them from simple giants is the oath — the bond sworn and kept at all costs. A Troll's word is their soul, and breaking it costs them physically as much as spiritually. They have survived the Sundering and the cracking of the Dreaming by doing what they have always done: standing between those they protect and everything that threatens them, and refusing to move.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through experiencing genuine awe, courage, or the honoring of oaths — 1 per scene where genuine valor is witnessed or demonstrated.

## Kith Powers

Powers build on each other. A character must have the previous level to purchase the next.

**●○○○○ Unyielding** *(Passive)* — physical presence is supernaturally imposing. Beings approaching with intent to intimidate or coerce must make a Willpower roll (diff 6) or find their resolve faltering. +2 dice to all Intimidation rolls.

**●●○○○ Oathbound Strength** *(1 Glamour)* — acting directly in defense of a sworn oath raises Strength by 2 for the scene. If the oath is broken while active — by anyone, including the Troll — it collapses immediately and the Troll takes 2 Bashing from the psychic rupture.

**●●●○○ Stone Wall** *(1 Glamour, diff 7)* — plants themselves, immovable by any physical force for one scene, including vehicles, telekinesis, supernatural strength. Cannot advance while active but may turn and strike freely. Roll Stamina + Athletics.

**●●●●○ Oath Sense** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — senses any broken oath within vicinity — not the content, but the fact and severity. Knows who broke it and roughly when. A being who has broken an oath to the Troll cannot lie to them for one week.

**●●●●● Giant's Might** *(2 Glamour, diff 8)* — becomes literally gigantic for one scene, doubling in size. All Physical Attributes +3. Damage dealt is Aggravated. Cannot be used in enclosed spaces without consequence. Veil violation near-certain in any human-populated area.

## Kith Gift & Kith Frailty

| Kith Gift: The Oath Keeper's Gift | Kith Frailty: The Cost of Betrayal |
|---|---|
| Cannot be forced to break a sworn oath by any supernatural compulsion — Dominate, Dementation, Fascination, or any other mind-affecting power cannot make them betray an oath.
