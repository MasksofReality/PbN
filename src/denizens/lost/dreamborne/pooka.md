*Shapeshifters of the Absurd — Those Whose Laughter Is the Sharpest Blade*

<p class="eyebrow">← Back to <a href="/denizens/lost/dreamborne/">Dreamborne</a></p>

## Who They Are

Pookas are shapeshifters, tricksters, and masters of the absurd, the Kiths who understand that the most devastating truth can be delivered by someone no one is taking seriously. Their animal affinities (each Pooka bonds to one specific animal at creation) shape their powers, and their compulsive inability to lie straight creates a mode of communication that is simultaneously maddening and more honest than most. In a world full of deadly earnestness, a Pooka's laughter is frequently the only honest sound in the room.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine laughter — their own or caused in others — and through successful misdirection that serves a purpose beyond mere entertainment.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Animal Affinity** *(Passive)* — a chosen animal form established at creation. Shifts freely at will, no Glamour cost, no roll, instantaneous. Retains full intelligence in animal form. Permanent.

**●●○○○ Trickster's Tongue** *(Passive)* — cannot tell a direct lie (see Frailty), but can make the truth sound like a joke and a joke sound like the truth. +3 dice to Subterfuge. Truth-detection against a Pooka must beat Manipulation + Expression or fail to determine what's genuine.

**●●●○○ Borrowed Face** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — takes on the appearance (not abilities) of any person observed for at least five minutes. Supernaturally accurate — voice, scent, mannerism. Lasts one scene. Roll Wits + Performance.

**●●●●○ The Convincing Absurdity** *(1 Glamour, diff 7)* — says something completely ridiculous and believes it so completely others begin to as well — a temporary false belief the target genuinely accepts for the scene. Roll Manipulation + Expression vs. target's Wits.

**●●●●● Wild Shape** *(2 Glamour, diff 8)* — takes the form of any closely observed animal, physically accurate, passes supernatural scrutiny at difficulty 8. Lasts one scene. May combine the bonded animal's form with one other simultaneously.

## Kith Gift & Kith Frailty

| Kith Gift: The Fool Who Sees Clearly | Kith Frailty: Cannot Lie Straight |
|---|---|
| Perceives manipulation, performance, and social theater with unnerving clarity — always knows when someone is performing a role, and the gap between performance and reality. Cannot be socially deceived by anyone playing a role. | Constitutionally incapable of speaking a direct, unambiguous lie. Every false statement comes out sideways — technically true in some absurd sense, obviously misleading, or a clear joke. Can mislead and confuse skillfully, but cannot state something known false with a straight face. Ever. |
