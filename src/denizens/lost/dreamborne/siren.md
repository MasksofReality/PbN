## Who They Are

Sirens, the Merfolk Kith, are the Dreamborne of water and dangerous desire. Their power is not straightforward seduction; it is the depth-pull, the current that moves people toward things they have not consciously chosen. The best Sirens are deeply intentional about this, using their ability to draw people toward truths, toward necessary confrontations, toward connections they have been avoiding. The worst use it purely instrumentally. Most fall somewhere between. In Basilica Purgatory they are most commonly found near the bay, near the Isle of Purgatory's waters, and in the deep flooded levels of the Undercity.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine moments of depth — being near open water, causing a being to confront something they had been avoiding, or creating a situation where genuine desire becomes the force that moves a situation forward.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Water's Ease** *(Passive)* — moves through water (or any liquid) at full speed with no penalties, holds breath indefinitely, maintains full function in cold, salt, or pressure conditions that would incapacitate a mortal. Senses water's presence within a wide radius.

**●●○○○ The Pull** *(1 Glamour)* — presence creates a subtle gravitational desire in those nearby — not for the Siren specifically, but toward whatever the target most genuinely wants — redirectable toward a chosen direction. Roll Charisma + Empathy (diff 6) vs. target's Willpower.

**●●●○○ Voice of the Deep** *(1 Glamour, diff 7)* — voice carries a resonance that bypasses conscious resistance, not mind control but direct address to desire. Whatever the Siren describes, the target finds genuinely appealing for the scene. Roll Charisma + Expression vs. target's Willpower.

**●●●●○ Siren's Call** *(2 Glamour, diff 8)* — issues a true Call, a directed desire-pull at one target. Target is drawn physically toward the Siren and cannot easily stop moving without succeeding at a Willpower roll (diff 8) each turn. Won't override survival instinct. Roll Manipulation + Occult.

**●●●●● The Deep's Claim** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — claims a being for the deep — compels them to confront their deepest, most avoided truth and act on it genuinely before the scene ends. Not harmful by design; removes avoidance as an option. Once per target, ever.
