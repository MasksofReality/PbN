## Who They Are

Sluagh are the Kiths of whispers and hidden things — beings who have cultivated an intimate relationship with the unseen, the suppressed, and the deliberately forgotten. They speak in whispers. Not because they cannot speak normally — they simply know that secrets travel on quiet breath. Their powers all rotate around the manipulation of perception: what is seen, what is known, what can be revealed or concealed. They are among the most feared Kiths because no one is ever certain what a Sluagh actually knows.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through acquisition of genuine secrets, successful acts of concealment on behalf of another, and time spent in the deep quiet of places others avoid.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Whisper Web** *(Passive)* — hears all whispered conversations within a large space clearly. Receives a faint psychic echo of significant secrets spoken anywhere in an occupied building within the last 24 hours. Roll Perception + Alertness (diff 4) to isolate specific conversations.

**●●○○○ Shadow Step** *(1 Glamour)* — moves in absolute silence, instinctively overlooked in shadows and peripheral vision. For one scene, Stealth rolls at -3 difficulty; attempts to notice or track at +2 difficulty.

**●●●○○ The Concealed Truth** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — makes a specific piece of information harder to recall, speak, or write down for a target — not forbidden, simply slipping away. Lasts one week. Roll Manipulation + Occult vs. target's Intelligence.

**●●●●○ Secret Extraction** *(2 Glamour, diff 8)* — reaches into a target's suppressed memories to draw out a specific piece of information, not by force but by creating a space where the secret wants to come out. Roll Perception + Empathy vs. target's Willpower. Target whispers the secret without fully realizing it.

**●●●●● Veil of the Unseen** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — makes a specific being, object, or location completely imperceptible to all witnesses simultaneously for a full day — not invisible, simply not noticed, remembered, or recorded. Photographs won't develop; recordings have gaps. Once per week.

| Kith Gift: The Archive of Whispers | Kith Frailty: The Whisper's Price |
