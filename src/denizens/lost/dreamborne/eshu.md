## Who They Are

Eshu are wanderers, storytellers, and crossroads-keepers of the Kiths who stand at the junction of all paths and know which road leads where. Their wisdom comes not from books or study but from experience accumulated across distances that would kill anyone who lacked their gift for being in the right place at the right time. In a world where the fifteen Basilicas are separated by lethal wilderness and most people die without traveling more than a mile from where they were born, the Eshu's knowledge of the roads is a form of power.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine acquisition of new knowledge, reaching a new place for the first time, or sharing a true story that changes someone's understanding of something.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ The Knowing Road** *(Passive)* — never gets lost, always knows approximately where they are relative to any destination visited within 500 miles. Senses when a path is dangerous before taking it (Perception + Survival, diff 4).

**●●○○○ Crossroads Lore** *(1 Glamour)* — at any junction, threshold, or significant meeting point, asks one question about a choice ahead and receives a true answer — not prophecy, knowledge that already exists. Roll Perception + Occult (diff 6).

**●●●○○ The Story That Opens Doors** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — tells a specific true story, even a short one, and the listener is compelled to share one piece of genuine information in return. Roll Charisma + Expression.

**●●●●○ Walk the Memory Road** *(2 Glamour, diff 7)* — travels through a location's historical memory, witnessing any significant event that occurred there, choosing the period. Complete and accurate. Requires five minutes of stillness. Roll Intelligence + Occult.

**●●●●● Weave the Crossroads** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — standing at a significant crossroads, reshapes which path is most accessible to a specific person or group. One path becomes easier and one harder for the next week. Cannot create paths that don't exist — only adjusts the weight of what's already possible.
