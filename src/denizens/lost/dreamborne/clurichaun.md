*The Necessary Disruption — Those Who Break the Right Rules at the Right Time*

<p class="eyebrow">← Back to <a href="/denizens/lost/dreamborne/">Dreamborne</a></p>

## Who They Are

Clurichaun are the wild cards, the troublemakers, and the liberators — Kiths whose purpose is to be the necessary disruption that prevents complacency from calcifying into injustice. They drink too much, fight at the best moments, make friends with everyone including people they disagree with, and have an unerring instinct for the moment when the established order needs to be kicked in the teeth. They are not reckless; the best Clurichaun are precisely reckless, aiming their chaos with care.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine acts of principled rebellion — breaking a rule that needed breaking, liberating someone from a situation they couldn't escape alone, or making someone laugh at something they were taking too seriously.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ The Lucky Break** *(Passive)* — once per scene, a botch or critical failure's consequence is transformed into something inconvenient but not catastrophic, and usually funny. The failure still happened; its worst results are redirected.

**●●○○○ Liquid Courage** *(1 Glamour)* — whether or not actually drinking, activates the benefits of optimum inebriation: +2 dice to Social rolls, immunity to fear effects for the scene, genuine sense that everything is probably fine. Not impaired — operating at peak Clurichaun.

**●●●○○ Rebellious Luck** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — directs wild fortune at a target — a blessing (next significant roll at -2 difficulty) or a curse (next significant roll botches on any failure). Choose before activation. Roll Wits + Subterfuge.

**●●●●○ Break the Chain** *(2 Glamour, diff 7)* — severs one specific supernatural compulsion, bond, or binding from a willing target — mild Blood Bonds, Dominated instructions, Contract obligations, Creed compulsions. Does not work on fundamental natures or curses from more powerful beings. Roll Willpower vs. the original effect's difficulty.

**●●●●● The Grand Liberation** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — causes a single rigid, oppressive system — legal structure, formal supernatural obligation, deeply embedded social contract — to crack visibly, opening space for change. Not destruction; weakening a load-bearing wall holding something unjust in place. Specific effect worked out with the Storyteller. Once per month.

## Kith Gift & Kith Frailty

| Kith Gift: Against the System | Kith Frailty: The Bar Tab |
|---|---|
| Cannot be bound by any formal system they haven't genuinely consented to — laws, regulations, supernatural obligations imposed without agreement, explicitly rejected social contracts all fail to hold with the usual force. Roll Willpower (diff 5) to ignore any externally imposed rule when relevant. | Luck is not unlimited, and chaos doesn't distinguish deserving disruption from undeserving. Once per session, the Storyteller may activate a consequence of a previous Clurichaun action that seemed fine at the time. Refusing to accept it costs 3 Glamour. |
