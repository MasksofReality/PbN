## Who They Are

Nockers are the craftspeople and engineers of the Dreaming — beings of obsessive creative energy who see the potential in every material and the flaw in every finished thing. They are brilliant, cantankerous, perpetually dissatisfied with everything they make (because they can always see how it could be better), and among the most genuinely valuable Kiths in a post-Sundering world where making things work is survival.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine creative breakthrough — completing a creation that works better than expected, solving an engineering problem no one else could, being given a challenging problem worthy of their gifts.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Flaw Sight** *(Passive)* — sees the flaw in everything: every machine, structure, plan, person. +3 dice to Investigation rolls to find weaknesses, malfunctions, or vulnerabilities. Cannot be turned off.

**●●○○○ Craft Beyond Craft** *(1 Glamour)* — a creation produced with this active is supernaturally superior to what materials and time allow — weapons deal +1 damage die, armor +1 soak, mechanical devices work at twice rated efficiency. Lasts until significantly damaged.

**●●●○○ Repair the Unrepairable** *(1 Glamour, diff 7)* — restores any broken, destroyed, or nonfunctional item to working condition in one scene of focused work, including items destroyed by supernatural means. Genuine and permanent. Cannot repair living beings. Roll Intelligence + Crafts.

**●●●●○ Dream-Forge** *(2 Glamour, diff 8)* — working with Glamour-infused materials, crafts a Token of Level 1–3 in a single extended session rather than the normal process. The Token is permanent and functional. One per week maximum.

**●●●●● The Perfect Flaw** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — identifies and exploits the single critical structural weakness of any target — machine, building, defensive position, magical power structure. By touching or studying for one turn, causes catastrophic failure: machines seize, buildings collapse at a specific point, magical constructs unravel from within. Once per scene.
