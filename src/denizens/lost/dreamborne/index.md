## Overview

Dreamborne are Fae beings who exist in a middle space between the mortal Lost and the fully supernatural Wyrd-born. They have survived the Sundering and the cracking of the Dreaming in ways specific to each Kith, and they arrived in Basilica Purgatory with purposes as varied as their natures.

Unlike the Lost, whose Seemings are shaped by their time in Arcadia, Dreamborne carry their Kith as their fundamental nature — they were not made into what they are by capture and escape. They are what they are because the Dreaming shaped them that way from the beginning. This makes their powers feel different from Lost Contracts: more inherent, less bargained, more an expression of self than an invocation of agreement.

## Glamour — The Dreamborne Resource

All Dreamborne powers are fueled by Glamour, the creative energy of the Dreaming that also sustains the Lost. Unlike Lost, who spend Glamour on Contracts, Dreamborne spend Glamour on Kith Powers, which function like Disciplines: activated by expenditure and roll, building from Level 1 through Level 5.

| | |
|---|---|
| Starting Glamour Pool | 10 for all Dreamborne |
| Maximum Glamour | 10 at creation; increases with Wyrd rating as the character advances |
| Replenishment | Kith-specific — each Kith's page describes what restores their Glamour, reflecting the nature of their archetype |
| Spending Glamour | As noted in individual powers. Level 1 powers often cost no Glamour; higher levels escalate. |
| Zero Glamour | Cannot activate any power; begins to feel the pull of the Dreaming's emptiness — a compulsion to seek Glamour-rich experiences that becomes increasingly difficult to resist |

## Kith Gift and Kith Frailty

Every Kith has a **Kith Gift** — a passive ability or significant advantage inherent to their nature — and a **Kith Frailty** — a genuine limitation that cannot be purchased away, mitigated by Merits, or overcome by any supernatural power. Both are expressions of the archetype, and both are permanent. The Frailty is not a punishment. It is the shape of what the Kith is.
