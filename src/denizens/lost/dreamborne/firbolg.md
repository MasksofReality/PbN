## Who They Are

Firbolg are the eldest of the Kiths in feeling, if not always in years, beings who carry the weight of having survived without the support others take for granted. Their archetype is the Orphan not as tragedy but as identity: they have found their own way, built their own foundation, and developed a self-sufficiency that cannot be taken from them because it was never given to them in the first place. In a post-Sundering world full of orphaned communities, displaced peoples, and beings who have lost everything they depended on, the Firbolg's hard-won resilience and their uncanny ability to find other survivors and build something new with them is one of the most powerful things any Kith can do.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine acts of self-sufficient survival — making do with less than expected, building community from scratch with people who have nothing, or being genuinely needed by someone who had no one else.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Survivor's Instinct** *(Passive)* — never fails to find food, shelter, and safety in any environment, even the hostile outside world. Survival rolls always at difficulty 3. Assesses any environment's resources and dangers immediately upon entering it, and always knows when in genuine danger before it manifests.

**●●○○○ The Hard Way** *(1 Glamour)* — accomplishes something with less than the normal required resources — half the tools, a fraction of the time, missing components filled by ingenuity. An extended action that would fail due to insufficient resources instead succeeds, though roughly. Roll Intelligence + relevant Craft or Survival (diff 6).

**●●●○○ Find the Others** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — senses other isolated, struggling, or abandoned beings within vicinity — lost children, abandoned community members, beings in crisis unable to ask for help, the deliberately isolated. Roll Perception + Empathy.

**●●●●○ Community From Nothing** *(2 Glamour, diff 7)* — establishes a genuine community bond between up to eight beings who share a common lack, struggle, or displacement. +2 dice on cooperative actions taken together; each member knows immediately when another is in genuine danger. Lasts one month; cannot be dissolved without unanimous consent.

**●●●●● We Were Always Here** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — plants roots, declaring a specific location as belonging to those with no other claim to belonging. For one year, the location is a genuine sanctuary for the displaced and orphaned — protected from supernatural harm, cannot be removed by force, basic needs mysteriously met. Does not require the Firbolg's presence to function once established. Once per year.

## Kith Gift & Kith Frailty

| Kith Gift: Cannot Be Broken | Kith Frailty: Cannot Ask for Help |
|---|---|
| Cannot be driven to despair by any supernatural means — no power, Gift, or Discipline can impose Harrowed, Broken, or Demoralized against their will.
