*Endless Possibility — Those Who See Wonder Where Others See Walls*

<p class="eyebrow">← Back to <a href="/denizens/lost/dreamborne/">Dreamborne</a></p>

## Who They Are

Piskies are the smallest, fastest, and most overlooked of the Kiths — and possibly the most dangerous for exactly that reason. Their archetype is the Child not because they are naive but because they retain the child's relationship with possibility: the genuine, undefended belief that what seems impossible might not be. In a world that has been telling everyone for two centuries that survival is the ceiling of aspiration, a Piskie's absolute certainty that the ceiling is wrong is a form of power that larger, stronger Kiths cannot replicate.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine discovery — finding something they didn't know existed, making someone experience wonder for the first time in a long while, or doing something everyone said was impossible.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Quicksilver** *(Passive)* — supernaturally fast and agile. +2 to all movement rates. Cannot be ambushed — always acts in the first initiative segment. Dodge roll difficulty -2.

**●●○○○ Spark of Wonder** *(1 Glamour)* — touches a mundane object, filling it with temporary magic — it glows faintly or briefly becomes beautiful in a way that stops observers. Mortals witnessing it make a Willpower roll (diff 6) or experience genuine wonder, restoring 1 Willpower. Lasts one scene.

**●●●○○ Tiny Door** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — passes through any gap large enough for a mouse — not by shrinking, but finding the space between spaces larger beings can't perceive. Lasts one scene. Roll Dexterity + Stealth. No space in Basilica Purgatory is truly closed to a Piskie using this.

**●●●●○ Impossible** *(1 Glamour, diff 7)* — attempts and succeeds at something that should be physically impossible given size, strength, or circumstance — catching a falling person, moving something ten times their weight, crossing a gap nothing should cross. Roll Dexterity + Athletics. One impossible thing per scene.

**●●●●● Begin Again** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — creates a genuine moment of fresh start, wiping away accumulated failure, grief, or impossibility from one target — not removed from the world, but removed from the target's relationship with it. Once per person, ever.
