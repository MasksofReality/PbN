## Who They Are

Enforcers maintain order in Basilica Purgatory — city watch officers, private security contractors, licensed bodyguards, and the informal muscle keeping the Undercity's more volatile districts from chaos. Not all are BTAC; many operate in spaces the BTAC doesn't effectively reach. In a city with five supernatural factions operating invisibly within its walls, Enforcers are often the first ordinary humans to notice something is wrong, even if they can't name what it is.

## Roleplaying the Enforcer

The best Enforcer characters have a clear personal sense of what order means to them — not just the BTAC manual, but what justice actually looks like in a city this complicated. That definition gets tested constantly. Enforcers are also the Mundane Human most likely to encounter the supernatural first: a wound that heals too fast, a face impossible to remember, a disturbance with no trace. How they respond is one of the most character-defining questions available to this Profession.
