## Who They Are

Incandescent Owls believe that their return from death is not a gift but an obligation — a second chance to make right what they failed the first time. They carry that failure with them and pursue wisdom, honor, and discipline with the intensity of people who know exactly what carelessness costs. They are the Hellion most naturally suited to Mentor, Judge, and institutional anchor roles. In a post-Sundering world where most old structures have collapsed, the Owl's insistence on law and tradition feels both antiquated and desperately necessary — someone needs to hold the line. They do not claim to be kind. They claim to be correct, and they work hard to prove correctness is what the world needs most.

## The Tenets

**The Eight Edicts:** Recognition of Hope · Virtuous Existence · Nurture · Purgation of the Wicked · Instruction · Law · Harmony · Transcend.

**The Fivefold Way:** The Way of Origin · The Way of Lineage · The Way of Integrity · The Way of Obligation · The Way of Propriety.

The Incandescent Owl carries thirteen active tenets at creation — the most demanding Dharma to follow faithfully. It rewards players who genuinely engage with it and punishes players who treat it as flavor text.
