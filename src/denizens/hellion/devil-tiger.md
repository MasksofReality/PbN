## Who They Are

Devil Tigers believe that pain is not the enemy — numbness is. Each moment of genuine anguish is a lesson, each cry of truth a crack in the armor of denial. They are not sadists for the sake of cruelty; they are surgeons who believe the world can only heal if it can be made to feel again, and they will apply the iron until it does. In the Third Era, their philosophy finds brutal validation in a world that swung too far the other way: traumatized into stillness, unable to feel joy or fury with equal depth. The Devil Tiger's work is to wake it back up, by whatever means necessary.

## The Tenets of Hellfire

Ride the demon, do not let it ride you · Glory in the fires of the flesh and the passions of the soul · Teach others to live joyfully through pain · Throw fear into the flames and encourage others to do likewise · Do not hesitate — act · Seek out the brightest embers and feed them until they become bonfires · Meditate upon the flash of passion, its afterglow, and the darkness that follows · Be cultured in your passions and magnificent in your purpose.
