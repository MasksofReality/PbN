## Who They Are

Cerulean Veils live in a world most people cannot see. Every object, every place, every living thing has a spirit, and the Veil can hear them all. This is not a gift they enjoy effortlessly — it is a discipline that requires constant attention, daily ritual, and genuine humility before things that most of the world has forgotten exist. They are careful and dignified in their dealings with people, and they do not indulge in food, drink, or physical excess — not because these things are wrong, but because they distract from the work. In Basilica Purgatory, the Veil finds both a natural home and a significant challenge: the Gauntlet is damaged and the spirit world bleeds through unstably, meaning more spirits are present, and more of them distressed. The Veil's work has never been more necessary — or more dangerous.

## The Tenets of Balance

Obey the will of the spirits, with understanding of why it matters · Perform the proper ceremonies to remain pure · Shun the defiled · Travel to absorb the world's full spiritual landscape · Pass wisdom to others freely · Help others surpass the shortcomings of the flesh · Renew your commitment to enlightenment each day · Observe your taboos rigorously.

## Taboos — What They Are and How They Work

A Taboo is an action that pleases or offends a specific type of spirit, built through the Veil's personal relationships. At character creation, choose three specific Taboos with your Mentor: two actions that please spirits you work with (and commit to performing regularly), and one that would offend your primary spirit contact (and must be avoided). Your Taboo list may grow as your Dharma rating does.
