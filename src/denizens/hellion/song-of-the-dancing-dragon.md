## Who They Are

Dancing Dragons are dead things in love with being alive. They consume experience with a Hunger that goes beyond metaphor — eating, drinking, fighting, creating, destroying, and connecting with an intensity that makes other Hellions uncomfortable and other supernaturals quietly envious. In their first lives, many denied themselves, whatever the reason. Death and return burned that out of them completely. They came back for the things they never allowed themselves to have, and they are not subtle about the pursuit. Beneath the celebration, the Hunger is never fully satisfied — the joy always chasing something it cannot quite catch. The Dancing Dragon knows this, and keeps dancing anyway. The alternative is admitting death won.

## The Tenets of Life

Attune yourself to life in all its forms · Do not shut out passion — experience it, learn from it, help others do the same · Use your power purposefully · Help all living things thrive; release what is genuinely dying beyond saving · Half-life is an abomination — be as alive as you can possibly be · Greet the world's renewal daily · Laugh often · Protect the spirits.

<div class="callout">
Of the five common Dharmas, the Dancing Dragon is the most broadly popular for OOC reasons — flexible mechanics, liberating philosophy. Should be played with genuine commitment to the Dharma's intensity and appetite, not as a justification for passivity.
</div>

## Starting Essence Balance

Yang-aspected — life force, passion, active engagement. **7 Yang / 3 Yin.**

## Dharma Disciplines

| Discipline | Level 1 Effect |
|---|---|
| **Flesh Shintai ●** | Living Skin — spend 1 Yang Essence for a complete living body (warm skin, heartbeat, breath) for one scene, passing casual supernatural scrutiny. Sunlight does not harm them while active. |
| **Yang Prana ●** | Vital Surge — spend 1 Yang Essence to add 2 dice to any Physical roll, declarable after seeing the pool. Once per scene at this level. |
| **Shapeshifting ●** | Subtle Shift — minor physical alterations (hair, facial structure, apparent age up to a decade) at will, no cost. Major changes cost 1 Yang Essence + a minute of concentration. |

<div class="callout callout--rare">
Sunlight Exception: while Flesh Shintai (Living Skin) is active, no sunlight damage — the only Dharma with this ability, at the cost of constantly spending Essence to maintain it during daylight. Dancing Dragons are also the only Hellion who can sire Dhampir children.
</div>
