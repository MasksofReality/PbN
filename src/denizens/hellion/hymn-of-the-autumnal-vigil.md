## Who They Are

Corpse Roses exist in the space between knowledge and understanding, between the dead and the living, between what can be known and what can only be felt. They collect, record, and study. They are not emotionless — they feel deeply, which is precisely why they've learned to govern feelings with such careful discipline. In life, many were melancholy and introspective. In death, that became something more useful: an absolute comfort with darkness, with the dead, with the parts of existence the living cannot face. The dead do not judge them, and in the Third Era, there are more dead than ever.

## The Tenets of Sunset

Bathe in the darkness, but hold fast to what remains of the light · Protect the bonds of family and community against corruption and loss · Tend to spirits in their places and the dead in their grief · Gather knowledge from every source available · Record what you have learned · Share what you know freely, but with intention · Look beyond what is obvious · Console the grieving and remind others to let the dead go.
