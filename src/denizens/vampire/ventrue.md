## Who They Are

Today's Ventrue have not forgotten what their arrogance cost. Like a phoenix from the ashes, they rise with respect for the past and clear eyes toward the future. They favor movers and shakers who make the Basilicas stronger — not the iron-fisted rulers of the old world, but the best in whatever path they have chosen. If others must be commanded to achieve that perfection, the Ventrue will use every tool available to them.

## Bloodline Disciplines

All Ventrue begin with one dot in each of their three Bloodline Disciplines, treated as in-clan for all experience costs.

| Discipline | Level 1 Effect |
|---|---|
| **Dominate ●** | Mesmerism — issue simple one-word commands (Stop, Run, Sleep, Drop) to a target met with eye contact. Resisted by Willpower (diff 7). Does not work on Lilim of equal or lower generation. |
| **Fortitude ●** | Endurance — add this dot to soak rolls against Lethal damage (normally Lilim cannot soak Lethal without Fortitude); also adds to soak against Aggravated. |
| **Presence ●** | Awe — radiates unconscious authority. +1 die on first-impression rolls with mortals, who find it difficult to dismiss or ignore the Ventrue without conscious effort. |
