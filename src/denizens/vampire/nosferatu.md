## Who They Are

The Nosferatu went from the dregs of Kindred society to its new kingmakers. The Sundering disfigured much of the world's population — in the entry camps at the Basilica outskirts, the Nosferatu can walk among diseased and altered survivors without breaking the Veil. They turned their love of information into an open business. They sit at tables the Ventrue cannot access, hold information the old Camarilla would have killed for, and know every secret in every basement in the city. It feels good to be the king.

## Bloodline Disciplines

| Discipline | Level 1 Effect |
|---|---|
| **Animalism ●** | Feral Whispers — communicate with and command animals, with particular affinity for rats, insects, and urban scavengers (command difficulty 5 for these). |
| **Obfuscate ●** | Cloak of Shadows — overlooked when still and quiet; observers need Perception + Alertness (diff 6) to notice. Given their appearance, this Discipline is not optional. |
| **Presence ●** | Awe (reframed) — projects unsettling gravity rather than beauty or authority. +1 die to Intimidation and information-gathering rolls; does not apply to charm-based interactions. |
