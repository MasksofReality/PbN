Five categories of notable items exist in Requiem by Night: **Fetishes & Talens** (Garou spirit-bound objects), **Tokens** (Lost Fae items), **Artifacts** (cross-splat magical items), **Relics** (historically significant objects), and **Devices** (Technocracy salvage and Omega Crate technology).

| Rating | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ✅ Available | Standard item — take it as a Background at character creation with Mentor sign-off |
| ⭐ Recommended | Strongly fits RbN's setting and themes |
| 🔶 Rare/Restricted | Exists but uncommon — additional background justification or in-play acquisition preferred |
| ⚠ ST Approval | Storyteller-placed — not available at character creation, acquired through play only |
| 🔧 House-Ruled | Adapted from source material with RbN-specific modifications |

## Fetishes & Talens — Spirit-Bound Objects of the Garou Nation

A **Fetish** is an object with a spirit permanently bound inside via a Rite; the spirit's nature determines its powers. A **Talen** is a single-use Fetish — more common, and freed on use. In RbN, the Gauntlet is damaged, so creating a new Fetish requires Storyteller involvement. The Garou bane is obsidian, not silver — any Fetish referencing silver has been adapted accordingly.

- **Harmony Flute** ✅ — Played, forces aggressive listeners to make a Rage roll or cease hostilities. Prized by Children of Eshtarra and Fianna diplomats.
- **Magpie's Swag** ✅ — A bag holding three times its apparent volume. Essential kit for Silent Striders.
- **Lie Detector Earring** ⭐ — Vibrates when the wearer is lied to verbally. Invaluable for Veil Keepers operatives.
- **Eternal Waterskin** ✅ — Always contains fresh water, regardless of environment.
- **Nyx's Bangle** ⭐ (Ragabash favored) — +4 dice to nighttime Stealth; eyes glow faintly, so plan accordingly.
- **Tick-Trace Fetish Radio** 🔶 (Glass Walkers favored) — Tracks targets in both material world and Penumbra; requires Storyteller coordination.
- **Apeskin** ⭐ (Metis and Lupus) — Suppresses obsidian vulnerability for a full day, letting the Garou pass fully as human.
- **Fang Dagger** ✅ (combat) — Doubles damage-roll successes on activation; aggravated damage. Mind the Veil outside controlled areas.
- **Partridge Wing** ✅ — Grants flight at running speed for one scene — valuable mobility in a vertically-layered city.
- **Spirit Whistle** 🔶 (Theurge favored) — Drives spirits away in a directional cone. ST involvement generally required for Penumbra scenes.

**Talens:** Nightshade Talen ✅ (turns the user to living shadow for a scene), Bane Arrow Talen 🔶 (aggravated damage to spirit/Wyrd-corrupted entities), Healing Talen ⭐ (heals one aggravated health level), Lune's Touch Talen 🔧 — since Luna is imprisoned in RbN, this draws on stored Lune-spirit energy rather than her direct blessing, making it significantly rarer; each requires a Storyteller-witnessed negotiation scene to create.

## Tokens — Objects of Fae Power for the Lost

Every Token has a **Drawback** (a small cost) and most have a **Catch** (a dread cost that activates the Token without spending Glamour). Thanks to proximity to the Isle of Purgatory — which remains half in the Dreaming — Tokens are more accessible in Basilica Purgatory than almost anywhere else. The Lost Mirror maintains an active Token trading economy.

- **Heartstring Thread** ✅ — Tied between two objects/people, reveals if either is moved, harmed, or lying. Used by faction members to monitor Dreaming gate integrity.
- **Goblin Coin** ✅ — Given only freely by a goblin; removes the Drawback from one Goblin Contract use.
- **Mien Cloak** ⭐ (Darkling, Fairest) — Resists being remembered by observers. Essential Veil maintenance for Lost whose Mien bleeds through.
- **Hedge Window** ⭐ — See through any other Hedge Window you've touched, for up to ten minutes. A key intelligence asset for gate monitoring.
- **Pledge Stone** ⭐ — Warms when held by an oath-keeper, cools for an oath-breaker. Important for diplomatic work between Lost, Dreamborne, and Wyrd-born.
- **Dreamcatcher Shield** 🔧 — Filters intrusive dream-fragment noise near unstable gates; +2 dice to Clarity rolls nearby. Adapted for RbN's cracked-open Dreaming.
- **Wyrd Brand** 🔶 — Marks the bearer as under a specific Wyrdborn's protection. Real protection, real obligation — requires ST approval and background integration.
- **Hedge-Spun Armor** ✅ (Ogre, Beast favored) — 1–3 points of armor woven from Hedge materials; may occasionally assert its own will near strong Wyrd energy.
- **Gate Key of the Breaking World** ⚠ (RbN Original) — Opens or closes any unsealed Arcadia gate. Not available at creation — a Storyteller reward for demonstrated service to the Dreaming's stabilization.

## Artifacts — Cross-Splat Magical Objects

Not tied to a single splat's power system — genuine supernatural items any appropriately-positioned character might encounter.

- **Mirror of Honest Reflection** ⭐ — Reveals emotional truth without compelling speech. A favorite of negotiators and Veil Keepers investigators.
- **The Chronicler's Seal** ⭐ (Archivists especially) — Documents sealed with it can't be falsified or destroyed. The Shadow Directorate would love to own it.
- **Veil-Glass** ⭐ (Veil Keepers, Hunters, Admins) — Reveals whether an observed individual is supernatural or has recently used supernatural ability.
- **The Weighing Bowl** 🔶 — Heats when either party in a sworn agreement intends deception. Used historically in Shadow Directorate treaty negotiations.
- **The Ossian Ledger** ✅ (Ossian Sorcerers, Archivists) — Writing in blood is permanently preserved; previous owners' entries can sometimes be read in dreams.
- **Sundering Shard** ⚠ (RbN Original, ST only) — A fragment of crystallized Wyrd energy that amplifies nearby abilities but attracts Wyrdstorm-born anomalies over time.

## Relics — Objects of Historical and Spiritual Significance

Unlike Artifacts, Relics become what they are through time and event rather than active construction.

- **Eshtarra's Tear** ⚠ (Children of Eshtarra especially) — A crystallized drop from Eshtarra's Ascension; boosts healing/restoration Gifts. Treated as sacred stewardship, not personal ownership.
- **Fragment of the Pattern Web** ⚠ (Sorcerers) — Lets a Sorcerer ignore one point of Difficulty on a school-based roll once per scene. The God Machine Guild would very much like to acquire one.
- **Lilith's Covenant Token** 🔶 (any Vampire bloodline) — Given at the moment of choosing rebirth; pulses with warmth during genuine spiritual crisis, reducing a Willpower roll's difficulty by 2.
- **Silent Strider Waystone** ✅ (Silent Striders primarily) — Senses direction and distance to other Waystones the same Garou has placed; limited impression-based information transfer.
- **The Ossian's Last Working** ⚠ (Ossian Sorcerers) — A sealed pre-Sundering ritual allowing one-time communication with a specific dead person. Irreversible, and it attracts attention.
- **Hellion Dharma Artifact** ✅ (Hellion, Dharma-specific) — An object absorbed into a Dharma's cultivation practice over long use; +1 die to cultivation rolls. Devil Tigers favor forged weapons; Incandescent Owls favor legal or ceremonial instruments; Hymn of the Autumnal Vigil practitioners favor ink and paper; Dancing Dragons favor percussion or performance instruments; Spirit of the Living Earth practitioners favor carved stone or living wood. Discuss the specific object and its history with your Mentor.

## Devices — Technocracy Salvage & Omega Crate Technology

Not supernatural, but their capabilities exceed what the Consensus deems possible. Primarily the domain of Ghostwrights, God Machine Guild members, and tech-focused characters across all splats.

- **Aether Resonance Detector** ⭐ (God Machine Guild, Ghostwrights) — Detects Aether Reactor signatures; notoriously inaccurate but invaluable for mapping hidden infrastructure. The Guild actively suppresses these.
- **Consensus Dampener** ⭐ (Psychics, Sorcerers) — Disrupts God-Machine Consensus monitoring in a small area for up to an hour. Actively hunted by the Guild; owning one is a known risk category.
- **Omega Crate Medical Unit** ✅ (Physicians, any) — A self-contained diagnostic/treatment unit; +2 dice to Medicine rolls. Legitimate, valuable, and non-suspicious.
- **Neural Interface Rig** 🔶 (Ghostwrights primarily) — +2 dice to Computer/Tech rolls when interfaced with Guild-compatible systems; carries a real risk of reverse-connection surveillance.
- **Wyrdstorm Shelter Pod** ✅ (all splats) — Deployable emergency shelter, 6 hours of full Wyrdstorm protection, single-use.
- **Reality Anchor Splinter** ⚠ (ST only) — A fragment somehow separated from a Reality Anchor — a theoretical impossibility that nonetheless exists. Dampens local Wyrdstorm effects; makes the Lost uncomfortable; communicates with other Anchors.
- **Omega Protocol Uplink** ⚠ (Ghostwrights, God Machine Guild) — A direct uplink to the Technocratic Union's deep-space facilities, offering advance manifest access to upcoming Crate drops. Enormous intelligence and economic leverage.

*The world ended. The things it left behind did not. What you carry into Basilica Purgatory tells its own story.*
