## Who They Are

Physicians are indispensable anywhere — and in Basilica Purgatory, where the outside world produces injuries the God Machine Guild's official medical infrastructure can't always address quickly, they're something more. Their knowledge extends beyond standard medicine: Omega Crate pharmaceutical compounds with only partially understood interactions, wounds made by things official medicine doesn't acknowledge, secrets about patients that would create serious problems if disclosed. Every faction maintains a quiet relationship with at least one Physician who can treat an oddly healing wound and not ask too many questions.

|---|---|

## Roleplaying the Physician

The most interesting Physician characters have an ethical framework extending beyond the Basilica's official medical guidelines. In a city where the God-Machine's infrastructure runs on something no one in medicine officially acknowledges, a Physician who asks the right questions eventually finds themselves in territory their training didn't prepare them for.
