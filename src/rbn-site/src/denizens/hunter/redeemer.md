## Who They Are

Redeemers carry something the other Hunters do not: personal knowledge of what it means to be on the wrong side of a line you cannot uncross. They've made serious mistakes and chosen to spend the rest of their life making it right — not because the math works out, but because it's what they have to do. Redeemers are not defined by their failure, but by what they do after it — and they often work in the spaces where the supernatural and human communities most need someone who can speak both languages.

## Mundane Hunter Benefits (All Levels)

| Level | Benefit |
|---|---|
| 1 | -1 difficulty on Empathy rolls |
| 2 | -2 difficulty negotiating with supernaturals wronged by Hunters — personal credibility |
| 3 | Can offer genuine assistance to supernaturals seeking to reform — a relationship of trust, not a power |
| 4 | Once per month, a supernatural or Hunter NPC seeks them out for help addressing a past transgression |
| 5 | Accomplishes something genuinely redemptive for someone else (worked out with ST) |
