## Who They Are

Zeal Hunters are the most complete expression of what the God-Machine intended when it created the Hunter mandate. They do not merely protect the balance — they embody it, holding the precise middle point where human and supernatural life can coexist. This is harder than it sounds: it requires standing against a Hunter who has gone too far as readily as against a supernatural who has violated the compact. The Shih tradition was built around Zeal, and its path is open to anyone willing to live it, regardless of origin.
