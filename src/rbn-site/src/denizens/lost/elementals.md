## Who They Are

Elementals are the most plentiful of all the Lost — living golems, the flame forever dancing in the pyre, the child who longed to be real. Whatever the reason, the human who became an Elemental was chosen for a single characteristic, and that one part of them became their entire identity to the Keeper. Once in the Dreaming, that element became everything. Their greatest fear is not pain or death — it is irrelevance. It is losing the one thing that made them worth taking.

## Seeming Contracts

Level 1 is shown below; Levels 2–5 escalate in cost and power — ask your Mentor for the full progression packet.

| Contract | Level 1 Effect | Catch |
|---|---|---|
| **Hearth ●** | Warm Presence — allies within arm's reach gain 1 automatic success on their first Willpower roll each scene. Passive, always on. | None required — always active. |
| **Elements ●** | Element Sense — senses the presence and state of their specific element within a wide radius. Element chosen at creation to match Seeming. | Free (automatic) when the element is actively threatening someone nearby. |
| **Cleansing ●** | Sense Taint — senses spiritual contamination or supernatural pollution in a person, place, or object, and roughly how severe it is. | Free (automatic) when touching something deeply suspicious. |

## Blessing & Curse

| Blessing: Lifebound | Curse: Inhuman |
