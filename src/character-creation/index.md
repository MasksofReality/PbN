Welcome to Basilica Purgatory. This guide walks you through every step of creating a character. Read it fully before beginning your application — your Mentor is there to help at every stage.

## How Character Creation Works

| Step | What You'll Do |
|---|---|
| **1. Choose your Splat** | The broad supernatural (or human) type you'll play |
| **2. Choose your Subtype** | The specific bloodline, tribe, seeming, dharma, school, or profession within your splat |
| **3. Assign Stats & Attributes** | Distribute your starting points across the character sheet |
| **4. Write your Background** | Your character's history, arrival in Basilica Purgatory, and ties to the world |
| **5. Submit your Application** | Reviewed by a Mentor, then by Admin, before approval |

<div class="callout">
Some subtypes are marked <strong>Rare</strong>. These require additional background detail and an extra Admin review before approval. First-time players are strongly encouraged to choose a common subtype.
</div>

## Choosing Your Splat

Basilica Purgatory supports eight playable splats. All splats except Hunter may join any of the five Factions.

| Splat | Type | Common Subtypes | Rare Path? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Vampire (Lilum)** | Supernatural | 6 Bloodlines | Yes — other bloodlines |
| **Werewolf (Garou)** | Supernatural | 6 Tribes | Yes — other tribes |
| **Lost** | Supernatural | 6 Seemings | Yes — Dreamborne Fae |
| **Hellion** | Supernatural | 5 Dharma paths | Yes — Thousand Whispers |
| **Sorcerer** | Supernatural | 6 Schools | No |
| **Psychic** | Human (Hidden) | 6 Types | No |
| **Human** | Mundane | 6 Professions + Half-Damned | No |
| **Hunter** | Mundane/Imbued | 4 Types | Yes — Bystander |

Full mechanics, strengths, weaknesses, and subtype breakdowns for each splat live on their own [Denizens](/#) pages: [Vampire](/denizens/vampire/) · [Werewolf](/denizens/werewolf/) · [Lost](/denizens/lost/) · [Hellion](/denizens/hellion/) · [Gifted (Sorcerer & Psychic)](/denizens/gifted/) · [Mortals](/denizens/mortals/) · [Hunter](/denizens/hunter/) · [Half-Damned](/denizens/half-damned/).

## Stats & Attributes

All characters use a modified version of the classic World of Darkness stat system. Your Mentor provides the full character sheet — the following outlines the core categories.

| Category | Attributes | Starting Points |
|---|---|---|
| Physical | Strength, Dexterity, Stamina | 1 free in each; 7 points to distribute |
| Social | Charisma, Manipulation, Appearance | 1 free in each; 5 points to distribute |
| Mental | Perception, Intelligence, Wits | 1 free in each; 3 points to distribute |
| Talents | Alertness, Athletics, Brawl, Dodge, Empathy, Expression, Intimidation, Leadership, Streetwise, Subterfuge | 13 points to distribute |
| Skills | Animal Ken, Crafts, Drive, Etiquette, Firearms, Melee, Performance, Security, Stealth, Survival | 9 points to distribute |
| Knowledges | Academics, Computer, Finance, Investigation, Law, Medicine, Occult, Politics, Science, Technology | 5 points to distribute |
| Backgrounds | Splat-specific — see Mentor | 5 points to distribute |
| Splat Traits | Disciplines / Gifts / Contracts / Essence / Schools / Psychic Ability / Profession Perks | See Mentor for splat-specific allocation |
| Willpower | All characters | Starts at 3 |
| Freebie Points | Spend after all above | 21 points — see cost chart with Mentor |

Supernatural splats have additional trait categories not listed above (Blood Pool, Gnosis, Glamour, Essence). Your Mentor will walk you through these specific to your splat.

## Writing Your Background

Your background is the most important part of your application — it tells us who your character is, how they survived to reach Basilica Purgatory, and why they're here.

**Required elements:**
- Who were they before? Life before the End of Times or during the Restoration.
- How did they survive? What did they endure, lose, or sacrifice to reach Year 200 TE?
- Why Basilica Purgatory? What brought them here specifically?
- What do they want? One short-term goal, one long-term goal.
- Who do they know? At least one NPC connection and one potential PC connection.
- What are they hiding? Every character has something they don't advertise.

| Path | Additional Requirement |
|---|---|
| Rare Subtype (any splat) | Full explanation of how it survived, how it functions today, and what it contributes to the Basilica. |
| Psychic (any type) | A detailed masking practice — how the character disguises their abilities as Sorcerer-style magic. |
| Dhampir | Explain parentage, how the character has survived given their rarity, and their relationship with both heritages. |
| Bystander Hunter | Explain what broke the Hunter's faith or cost them their abilities, and why they remain in the Basilica. |
| Dreamborne Fae | Explain survival, what service they provide their reigning Wyrd-born, and why they're inside the walls. |

## The Approval Process

| Stage | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **1. Mentor Review** | Submit your sheet and background. Your Mentor reviews within 72 hours. |
| **2. Revision (if needed)** | Most applications need at most one round of revision. |
| **3. Admin Review** | Once your Mentor approves, Admin reviews for sim balance, rare path approval, and integration with active storylines. |
| **4. Approval & Welcome** | You receive your character tag, faction introduction, and a starting IC scene from your Mentor. |

<div class="callout callout--rare">
Rare path applications go through an additional Admin review step and may take longer. Plan accordingly.
</div>

**Application checklist:**
- Completed character sheet with all stats filled in
- Background covering all six required elements
- Splat and subtype clearly identified
- Faction preference noted (can be undecided)
- Rare path supplemental background, if applicable
- Psychic masking practice described, if applicable
