*Telepathy & Mediumship — Hearing the Living and the Dead*

<p class="eyebrow">← Back to <a href="/denizens/gifted/">Gifted</a></p>

## Overview

Empaths combine Telepathy — perceiving and projecting into living minds — with Mediumship — perceiving and communicating with the dead. What unites these is listening: the Empath hears things never meant to be heard, from beings both present and absent, living and dead. The gift does not discriminate. Neither do the voices.

## Starting Attribute Priority

| Category | Priority | Starting Points |
|---|---|---|
| Social | Primary | 7 points |
| Mental | Secondary | 5 points |
| Physical | Tertiary | 3 points |

## Masking Practice

Empaths typically mask as Ossian or Augur Sorcerers — carrying ritual components associated with spirit communication and performing visible contact rituals before relaying received information.

## Abilities

Choose one option at each level as you advance. The choice at each level is permanent.

**●○○○○ Level 1 — Choose One**

*Option A — Emotional Read:* Passively sense the dominant emotion of any nearby person or spirit. Always active. +2 dice on Empathy rolls.

*Option B — Dead Sense:* Passively detect the presence of Ghosts and Wraiths nearby. Always active.

**●●○○○ Level 2 — Choose One**

*Option A — Surface Thoughts:* Focus on one person for a turn and receive their active surface thoughts. Roll Perception + Empathy (diff 6).

*Option B — Spirit Communication:* Speak directly with any Ghost or Wraith in range. Roll Perception + Occult (diff 6).

**●●●○○ Level 3 — Choose One** *(1 Willpower)*

*Option A — Thought Projection:* Project a thought, image, or message into a target's mind. Doesn't compel action. Roll Manipulation + Empathy (diff 7) vs. target's Willpower.

*Option B — Full Mediumship:* Enter genuine two-way communication with a specific dead person. Roll Charisma + Occult (diff 7).

**●●●●○ Level 4 — Choose One** *(1 Willpower)*

*Option A — Deep Read:* Access memories, beliefs, and genuine intentions of a target. They may sense intrusion (Willpower diff 8). Roll Perception + Empathy (diff 8).

*Option B — Channel:* Allow a willing spirit to speak through the Empath's own voice for the scene.

**●●●●● Level 5 — Choose One** *(2 Willpower)*

*Option A — Mind Touch:* Plant a belief or memory that feels completely authentic and permanent. Roll Manipulation + Empathy (diff 9) vs. target's Willpower.

*Option B — Summon the Dead:* Call a specific dead person and compel their presence for the scene. Roll vs. target's Willpower (diff 9).

## Strength & Weakness

| Strength: The Voices Are Always There | Weakness: The Voices Are Always There |
|---|---|
| +2 dice to see through performed emotion or deliberate social deception, always active. | Crowds are genuinely painful. -1 die on Mental rolls with 10+ people present from sensory overload. Spirits nearby know they can be perceived. |
