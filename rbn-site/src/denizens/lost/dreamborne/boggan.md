## Who They Are

Boggans are the community-builders of the Dreaming, small, practical, and possessed of a gift for making spaces feel like home and making people feel genuinely seen. They are the Kiths others underestimate most reliably, and the ones whose absence is felt most keenly when they leave. In a post-Sundering world of displaced people living inside walled cities, the Boggan's ability to create genuine community from nothing is among the most powerful things a Dreamborne can do.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine acts of community care — helping someone in real need, creating a space that feels safe, being trusted with something precious to someone else.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Sense Need** *(Passive)* — always knows what another being needs most in this specific moment, not what they want, but what would actually help. Emotional attunement, always accurate. +2 dice to Empathy rolls.

**●●○○○ Warm Hearth** *(1 Glamour)* — consecrates a space with genuine comfort. All beings within recover 1 Willpower per scene of rest; difficulty of hostile social actions within the space +2. Lasts one week.

**●●●○○ The Invisible Hand** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — completes any domestic, community, or maintenance task in a fraction of the normal time, without being noticed doing it. Roll Dexterity + Crafts.

**●●●●○ Hearth Bond** *(1 Glamour, diff 7)* — establishes a genuine community bond between up to five beings — not compulsion, genuine mutual care the Dreaming reinforces. +1 die on all rolls to help or protect each other; each member immediately knows when another is in danger. Lasts one month.

**●●●●● Community's Strength** *(2 Glamour, diff 8)* — channels collective emotional support into one person or the group. One target heals all Bashing and half Lethal damage immediately, or the entire group recovers full Willpower and gains +2 dice for the scene. Requires a genuine established community present.

## Kith Gift & Kith Frailty

| Kith Gift: The Caregiver's Sight | Kith Frailty: The Caregiver's Compulsion |
|---|---|
| Cannot be deceived about the genuine emotional needs of those around them — performed contentment and forced smiles are transparent. Always knows when someone is suffering whether or not it's shown. | Cannot ignore genuine suffering in their vicinity. When someone nearby is in real distress, a Willpower roll (diff 7) is needed to choose not to help immediately. On failure, stops whatever they were doing to attend to the suffering person. Enemies use this against them constantly. |
