## Who They Are

Satyrs are the Kiths of passion, music, and the hunger for genuine connection — beings who feel everything with an intensity that most people cannot sustain and do not always want to. Their power comes from that intensity, not from its expression. A Satyr who has chosen to channel their passion into art, into community, into the careful building of real relationships is among the most powerful social forces in the Dreaming. Their gifts are not about seduction — they are about making real contact, and the raw force of that contact affecting the world.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine passionate engagement — creating or experiencing music or art that moves them, making a genuine emotional connection with another person, or feeling something with their full capacity rather than managing it at a distance.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Passion Sense** *(Passive)* — feels the passionate drives of those around them as clearly as their own — desires, loves, hates, creative impulses. +2 dice to Empathy. Anyone lying about what they actually want registers as obviously false.

**●●○○○ The Music That Moves** *(1 Glamour)* — while performing (music, dance, poetry, any passionate art), willing listeners are moved to feel what the Satyr channels — authentic, not manufactured. Lasts the scene. Unwilling targets must beat the Satyr's Expression + Charisma with Willpower (diff 7) to remain unaffected.

**●●●○○ Passionate Connection** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — establishes a genuine emotional bond with one target — not necessarily romantic, intimate in the sense of genuine mutual recognition. For one month, each senses the other's dominant emotional state at any distance; Social rolls between them at -2 difficulty. Target always aware and consenting.

**●●●●○ Inspire** *(1 Glamour, diff 7)* — channels passion into one target's creative or passionate act — a fight, speech, creation, expression of love. Target gains +3 dice on the roll and the action carries supernatural force. Roll Charisma + Expression.

**●●●●● The Great Passion** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — accesses fullest capacity for feeling, creating an area where masks and performed feelings are impossible for one scene. All beings feel authentic emotions and cannot suppress or perform. Cannot be used harmfully — simply reveals what's already true. Once per session.

## Kith Gift & Kith Frailty

| Kith Gift: The Lover's Sight | Kith Frailty: The Passionate Flaw |
|---|---|
| Cannot be emotionally deceived — always knows the genuine feeling beneath the performed one, and whether a connection offered is genuine or transactional. Cannot be countered by any social skill or supernatural power. | Feels everything intensely and cannot modulate this. In situations of genuine emotional significance, a Willpower roll (diff 6) is needed to not respond with full emotional capacity immediately — the person who cries openly at the funeral while everyone else holds it together. |
