## Who They Are

Sidhe are the closest thing the Dreaming has to nobility — beings of such inherent authority and presence that even other Kiths who resent them acknowledge the weight of their bearing. They carry the Dreaming's political structures in their blood. This is not arrogance in most Sidhe, it is a genuine and sometimes painfully felt responsibility. The Leader archetype is not about domination; it is about being the person others look to when everything goes wrong, and not flinching.

## Glamour Replenishment

Glamour replenishes through genuine acts of leadership — making a decision that benefits those who depend on them at personal cost, rallying people who have given up, or being acknowledged as a leader by those genuinely free to refuse.

## Kith Powers

**●○○○○ Noble Bearing** *(Passive)* — presence commands automatic respect. Social first impressions in their vicinity are at one less difficulty. Hostile beings must make a Willpower roll (diff 5) before the first aggressive action against the Sidhe.

**●●○○○ The Commanding Voice** *(1 Glamour)* — a direct instruction carries supernatural weight; listeners must beat a Willpower roll (diff 7) or comply with a single reasonable directive. Cannot compel suicide, self-harm, or actions against deepest values. Roll Charisma + Leadership.

**●●●○○ Rally** *(1 Glamour, diff 6)* — all allied beings in the scene regain 2 Willpower and recover from Shaken or Demoralized immediately, acting with contagious confidence for the rest of the scene.

**●●●●○ Dreaming Authority** *(2 Glamour, diff 8)* — invokes the Dreaming's full authority behind their leadership. For one scene, no being under their formal authority can be compelled, Dominated, Demented, or otherwise supernaturally coerced against the Sidhe's stated will. Requires established actual leadership.

**●●●●● The Final Word** *(2 Glamour, diff 9)* — a binding declaration carrying the Dreaming's sanction. All Kiths and Lost who hear it are bound to honor it for one month. Other supernaturals must beat a Willpower roll (diff 9) or feel genuine compulsion to comply. Mortals hear it as the most reasonable instruction they've ever received. Once per month.
